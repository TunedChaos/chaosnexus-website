1. **Human direction** of the local-first MCP host, Rhai sandbox, Forge approval workflow, and dual licensing.
2. **Human review** of assisted drafts, especially security-sensitive code.
3. **Contributor responsibility** for copyright and AGPL / dual-license compliance (see [Licensing](/guide/licensing) and each repo’s `CONTRIBUTING.md`).
